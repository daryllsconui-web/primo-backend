ASHITABA

Ashitaba (Scientific:Name: Angelica
Keiskei Koidzumi) is a large herb that
grows primarily in the central region of
Japan. Its root, leaf, and stem are used to
make medicine

In Japan, many have long believed that
ashitaba is important in supporting a
person’s health and well-being.
Researchers are suggesting that a
compound in this plant boosts cellular
health and may prolong youth.
