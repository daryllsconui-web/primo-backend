The core ingredient in Ashi Supreme is Ashitaba
(Angelica keiskei). Its name literally translates to
"Tomorrow Leaf" in Japanese.

This comes from its legendary regenerative ability:
“If you pick a leaf from the plant today, a new one
often begins to sprout by the very next morning’.
