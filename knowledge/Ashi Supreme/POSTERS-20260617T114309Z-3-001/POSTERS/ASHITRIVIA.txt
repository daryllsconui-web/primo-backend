The Secret of "Chalcones"

The most scientifically interesting part of Ashitaba is a group
of organic compounds called Chalcones.

These are found in the yellow sap of the stem and are rare
in the plant kingdom.

They are highly prized for their ability to act as
natural antioxidants and support metabolic health.
