WHAT IS ASHI SUPREME?
Ashi Supreme is a mineral and vitamin-rich health drink by Supremacy International. It features ashitaba leaves sourced from Japan and is designed to support daily dietary needs and overall well-being.

KEY INGREDIENT: ASHITABA
Ashitaba (Angelica keiskei) is a fast-growing green plant native to Japan, known for its powerful health benefits. It has been used in traditional Japanese wellness for centuries. Ashitaba contains:
- Chalcones (powerful antioxidants unique to ashitaba)
- Vitamins B6, B12, and other B vitamins
- Vitamin C and vitamin E
- Minerals including calcium, potassium, and iron
- Chlorophyll and flavonoids
