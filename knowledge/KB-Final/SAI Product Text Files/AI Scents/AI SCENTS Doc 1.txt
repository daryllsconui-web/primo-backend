================================================== 3. FRAGRANCE PYRAMID
==================================================

Top Notes - First impression - Usually noticeable for 5–15 minutes -
Often citrus, aromatic or fresh

Heart Notes - Main body of the fragrance - Usually noticeable for 20–60
minutes - Often floral, fruity or spicy

Base Notes - Long-lasting foundation - Can remain for 6 hours or more
depending on skin chemistry, environment and application
