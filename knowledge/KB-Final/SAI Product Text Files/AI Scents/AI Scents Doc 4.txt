What are Top Notes? The first scent noticed after spraying. Usually
lasts about 5–15 minutes.

What are Heart Notes? The main character of the fragrance. Often
noticeable for 20–60 minutes.

What are Base Notes? The foundation of the fragrance and may remain for
several hours depending on usage and conditions.

Factors affecting longevity: - Skin type - Temperature - Humidity -
Activity level - Application amount - Fragrance concentration

Storage: - Keep away from heat. - Avoid direct sunlight. - Close cap
tightly. - Store in a cool, dry place.

Application Tips: - Spray onto clean, dry skin. - Apply to pulse
points. - Do not rub wrists together after spraying. - Avoid eyes and
broken skin.

FAQ

Q: Can perfume smell different on each person? A: Yes. Skin chemistry
and environment influence how a fragrance develops.

Q: Can I layer fragrances? A: Yes, if the scent profiles complement each
other.

Compliance: Do not claim medical, therapeutic, mood-altering, or
guaranteed social effects. Avoid guaranteeing longevity or attraction.
