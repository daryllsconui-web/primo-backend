==================================================

Category: Luxury Fragrance / Eau de Parfum
Size: 80ml / 2.7 FL OZ

- Maison Supreme geometric logo embossed/printed on the bottle — an ornate interlocking "S" inside a diamond-square frame, in gold
- "MAISON SUPREME" and "EAU DE PARFUM 80ML / 2.7 FL OZ" printed in gold text on the bottle

BOX/PACKAGING:
- Cylindrical matte black tube box (not a standard rectangular box — it is round/cylinder shaped)
- Gold geometric Maison Supreme logo centered on the front
- "MAISON SUPREME" in gold serif lettering below the logo
- "EAU DE PARFUM" and "80 ML / 2.7 FL OZ." in gold text at the bottom
- Overall aesthetic: dark luxury, high-end, editorial — evokes Middle Eastern/European niche perfumery

OVERALL LOOK:
- Premium, luxury positioning
- Black and gold color palette throughout
- The cylindrical box paired with the clear glass bottle creates a strong visual contrast
- Suitable for gifting — the packaging alone looks like a luxury item
