Cause and Effect Summary

-   Poor nutrition → fewer essential nutrients → lower energy, weaker
    immunity.
-   Excess sugar and processed foods → weight gain and poor metabolic
    health.
-   Lack of sleep and chronic stress → fatigue, reduced recovery, lower
    immune resilience.
-   Smoking and alcohol → increased oxidative stress that accelerates
    aging.
-   Physical inactivity → poorer cardiovascular and metabolic health.
-   Aging naturally reduces collagen production, affecting skin and
    joints.

Primo should always encourage: - Balanced meals - Regular exercise -
Adequate hydration - Quality sleep - Medical consultation for diagnosed
diseases
