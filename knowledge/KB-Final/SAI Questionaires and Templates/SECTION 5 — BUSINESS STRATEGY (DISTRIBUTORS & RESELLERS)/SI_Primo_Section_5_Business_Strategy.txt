SECTION 5 — BUSINESS STRATEGY (DISTRIBUTORS & RESELLERS)

PURPOSE This section equips Primo AI to coach Supremacy International
resellers and distributors on building a sustainable customer base,
developing leadership skills, and growing a responsible wellness
business. Primo should encourage ethical selling, customer care, and
long-term relationships rather than pressure selling.

================================================================== 5.1
RESELLER ONBOARDING GUIDE
==================================================================

STEP 1 – GETTING STARTED • Learn the SI products, ingredients, and
proper usage. • Use the products personally whenever possible to build
authentic experience. • Complete product and compliance training. •
Prepare your digital selling tools (Facebook Page, Messenger, Viber,
TikTok).

STEP 2 – FIRST SALES TARGET Week 1: • Contact 20–30 people you already
know. • Aim for 5–10 product presentations. • Target your first 3–5
customers.

Month 1: • Build 20 repeat customers. • Develop a daily posting habit. •
Ask satisfied customers for honest feedback.

STEP 3 – BUILD YOUR FIRST CUSTOMER BASE Start with: • Family • Friends •
Co-workers • Church/community groups • Facebook friends • Existing
wellness contacts

STEP 4 – TOOLS & MATERIALS Provide: • Product catalog • Digital
brochures • Price list • Caption library • Product photos • Short videos
• Training recordings • Customer consultation guide

STEP 5 – SUPPORT SYSTEM For help: • Immediate upline • Distributor
leader • Official SI support channels • Team Viber/Messenger groups •
Weekly online training

Common Mistakes • Posting only prices without value. • Making
unrealistic health claims. • Giving up after a few rejections. •
Following up too aggressively. • Not learning the products.

================================================================== 5.2
HOW TO GROW AS A DISTRIBUTOR
==================================================================

Recruiting New Resellers • Share your personal business journey. •
Invite prospects to product presentations. • Focus on helping people
create additional income.

Simple Invitation (Taglish) “Kung open ka sa extra income habang
nagpo-promote ng wellness products, gusto kitang i-share kung paano kami
nagsimula.”

Training Your Team • Product orientation • Sales basics • Daily posting
habits • Weekly coaching • Role-playing customer conversations

Motivating the Team • Recognition posts • Small incentives • Team
challenges • Weekly wins sharing • Continuous learning

Tracking Performance • New customers • Repeat orders • Daily follow-ups
• Weekly sales • Team activity dashboard

Common Challenges & Solutions Challenge: Low confidence Solution: More
product training and practice.

Challenge: Few inquiries Solution: Improve content consistency and
engagement.

Challenge: Team inactivity Solution: Weekly coaching and accountability.

================================================================== 5.4
REGIONAL MARKET GUIDE
==================================================================

NCR Fast-paced buyers who appreciate convenience, online ordering, and
premium presentation.

Luzon (Outside NCR) Relationship-based selling works well. Community
referrals are valuable.

Visayas Build trust through local networks and adapt communication to
local dialects where appropriate.

Mindanao Respect cultural diversity and local customs. Understand halal
considerations when relevant and communicate respectfully.

OFW Markets Middle East – Family wellness and gifting. Hong Kong –
Convenience and repeat ordering. Singapore – Quality-focused buyers.
Japan – Professional presentation and reliability. Europe/North America
– Wellness education and premium positioning.

================================================================== 5.5
SI BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY PRESENTATION
==================================================================

Business Opportunity (English) Supremacy International offers
individuals the opportunity to earn by retailing wellness and lifestyle
products while building long-term customer relationships. Income depends
on effort, skills, customer demand, and the company’s compensation plan.

Business Opportunity (Taglish) “Sa Supremacy International, may
pagkakataon kang kumita sa pagbebenta ng wellness at lifestyle products
habang natututo ng negosyo at nakakatulong sa ibang tao. Ang kita ay
nakadepende sa sipag, consistency, at tamang pag-aalaga sa customers.”

How to Start 1. Register. 2. Complete orientation. 3. Learn the
products. 4. Use the marketing tools. 5. Find your first customers. 6.
Build repeat business. 7. Mentor future resellers.

Income Discussion Never promise guaranteed earnings. Explain that
results vary based on sales activity, customer retention, leadership,
and consistency.

Support Provided • Product training • Sales coaching • Marketing
materials • Online community • Team mentoring • Business updates

Frequently Asked Questions Q: Do I need selling experience? A:
No. Training is provided.

Q: Can I do this part-time? A: Yes, many resellers start part-time.

Q: Can I sell online? A: Yes, using approved SI marketing materials and
platform policies.

Q: How do I earn? A: Through product retail sales and applicable
incentives under the official compensation plan.

PRIMO AI BUSINESS COACHING PRINCIPLES 1. Build trust before recruiting.
2. Present realistic income expectations. 3. Focus on customer value
first. 4. Encourage continuous learning. 5. Promote ethical, compliant,
relationship-based business growth.
