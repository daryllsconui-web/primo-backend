SECTION 4 — PHILIPPINE MARKETING MASTERY

PURPOSE This section trains Primo AI to coach resellers and distributors
on how to market Supremacy International products effectively using
Philippine platforms, buyer behavior, and culture. Marketing should
educate, build trust, and encourage responsible wellness practices.

======================================================== 4.1 FACEBOOK
MARKETING STRATEGY
========================================================

Best Post Types • Educational wellness tips • Product ingredient
highlights • Customer testimonials (with appropriate disclaimers) •
Lifestyle photos and reels • Behind-the-scenes content • FAQ posts •
Short videos • Before-and-after stories only when truthful and compliant
• Community activities • Live Q&A sessions

Best Time to Post (Philippines) • 7:00–9:00 AM • 12:00–1:00 PM •
7:00–10:00 PM

Facebook Live Tips 1. Introduce yourself. 2. Share one wellness topic.
3. Explain one featured product. 4. Demonstrate proper usage. 5. Answer
audience questions. 6. Invite viewers to message privately.

Facebook Group Strategy • Create a niche wellness community. • Share
daily value before selling. • Encourage members to ask questions. •
Celebrate customer milestones. • Post testimonials responsibly. • Run
weekly giveaways and educational challenges.

Paid Ads Advice • Start with ₱200–₱500/day. • Promote educational videos
and testimonials. • Target adults 25–55. • Optimize for Messenger
inquiries.

======================================================== 4.2 TIKTOK
STRATEGY ========================================================

Content Ideas • 30-second product education • Morning wellness routines
• Ingredient spotlights • Myth vs Fact videos • Customer stories •
Day-in-the-life wellness habits • Packing wellness kits for travel

Hook Ideas • “Alam mo bang…” • “Ito ang naging daily habit ko…” • “3
bagay na natutunan ko tungkol sa wellness…” • “Kung busy ka araw-araw,
panoorin mo ito.”

TikTok Shop Tips • Use clear product photos. • Write searchable titles.
• Add lifestyle videos. • Respond quickly to inquiries. • Encourage
satisfied customers to leave reviews.

Trending Formats • POV videos • GRWM (Get Ready With Me) •
Day-in-the-Life • Voice-over storytelling • FAQ videos

======================================================== 4.3 VIBER &
MESSENGER GROUP SELLING
========================================================

How to Start a Group 1. Create a group with a clear name. 2. Invite
customers and friends. 3. Post community rules. 4. Introduce yourself.
5. Share value daily.

Daily Content Calendar Monday – Wellness Tip Tuesday – Product Education
Wednesday – Customer Story Thursday – FAQ Friday – Live Reminder
Saturday – Promo Sunday – Inspirational Message

Engagement Ideas • Polls • Trivia • Referral contests • Birthday
greetings • Wellness challenges

Sample Welcome Message

“Kumusta! Welcome sa ating Supremacy Wellness Community. Dito tayo
nagbabahagi ng wellness tips, product updates, promos at customer
experiences. Feel free to ask questions anytime. Masaya kaming
makatulong!”
