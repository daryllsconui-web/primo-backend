Backstory | Developed for coffee lovers seeking a wellness-oriented coffee.

Supreme Alkaline Coffee Mix Premium Blend was developed for individuals who love coffee but are also looking for a more wellness-oriented beverage to complement their daily lifestyle. Rather than creating an ordinary instant coffee, Supremacy International formulated this product by combining premium coffee with carefully selected botanical ingredients such as Garcinia Cambogia, Mangosteen, Moringa, Tongkat Ali, and Alkaline Powder in one convenient sachet.

The philosophy behind the product is to provide coffee lovers with a premium coffee experience while incorporating a carefully selected botanical formulation into their daily routine. The product is designed to complement a balanced diet, regular physical activity, adequate hydration, and healthy lifestyle habits. It is intended as a food supplement and not as a medicine, making it suitable for individuals seeking a convenient wellness beverage without sacrificing the rich taste and aroma of coffee.
