What is Ashi Supreme? | A: Ashi Supreme is a plant-based food supplement formulated with Pure Ashitaba (Angelica keiskei) and carefully selected botanical ingredients such as Mangosteen, Goji Berry, Moringa, Buah Merah, and Soursop. Q6. Can I take Ashi Supreme together with other Supremacy products? | A: Many customers include Ashi Supreme as part of their daily wellness routine together with other official Supremacy products. Follow the recommended serving for each product. If you have medical concerns or take maintenance medication, consult your healthcare professional before adding food supplements.
Q7. When can I expect results? | A: Every individual is different. Experiences may vary depending on consistency of use, balanced nutrition, exercise, sleep, hydration, and overall lifestyle habits. Ashi Supreme is intended to complement healthy living rather than provide guaranteed outcomes.
Q8. Can I take Ashi Supreme together with prescription medicines? | A:  If you are taking prescription medicines, have an existing medical condition, are pregnant, or are breastfeeding, it is advisable to consult your licensed healthcare professional before using any food supplement.
Q9. Is Ashi Supreme suitable for senior citizens? | A:  Many adults, including senior citizens, choose food supplements as part of their wellness routine. Seniors with existing medical conditions or maintenance medications should consult their healthcare professional before using any food supplement.
Q10. How should I store Ashi Supreme? | A:  Store the product in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Always keep it in its original packaging and out of reach of children.
Q11. What makes Ashi Supreme different from other botanical supplements? | A:  Ashi Supreme combines Pure Ashitaba with carefully selected botanical ingredients in one formulation. Rather than relying on a single ingredient, the product emphasizes the synergy of multiple plant-based ingredients designed to complement one another as part of a healthy lifestyle.
Q12. Can Ashi Supreme replace a healthy diet? | A: No. Ashi Supreme is intended to complement, not replace, a balanced diet, regular exercise, proper hydration, and healthy lifestyle habits.
