HOW TO USE

(SIMPLE FIVE EASY STEPS )

@ STEP 1:

Wet your skin:
Thoroughly wet your face and/or
body with warm water.

@ STEP 2:

Lather the soap:

Rub the Supreme Radiance soap
between your hands or directly onto
your skin to create a lather.

@ STEP 3:

Massage gently:

Apply the lather to your skin, focusing
on areas where you want to see
improvement (like dark spots or
blemishes), and massage in gentle
circular motions for a few minutes.

@ STEP 4:

Rinse thoroughly:
Rinse off all the soap residue with water.

@ STEP 5:

Pat dry:
Gently pat your skin dry with a soft
towel, avoiding harsh rubbing.
